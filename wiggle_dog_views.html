①
Dog Card — the row
Always visible in the group list. No tap needed — scan and go. Name + address + door code + state dot. Never hides content, even in interlock (compact) mode. This is what the walker sees walking down the street.
②
Inline Expand — the shortcut
Tap the caret ▼ on a Dog Card. Quick actions drop in below without leaving the group screen. Pickup or add note — one hand, one tap, stay in the flow. Not for everything — just the most urgent micro-action at the door.
③
Dog Profile Drawer — the control centre
Tap the dog's name. Full bottom sheet. Every action and piece of information about this dog lives here. Pickup, Not Walking, Owl notes, Forever notes, owner, directions. This is where all future dog actions go — no exceptions.
