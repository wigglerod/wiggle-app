2 — Warn Next Walker — How It Works End to End
One toggle. Two writes. Next walker is protected.
When a walker sees something the next walker needs to know — reactive behaviour, building issue, address change — they flip the toggle. The note becomes both a permanent activity record AND a timed owl note that walks on the card.
①
Walker taps ✎ Note on Pebbles after the walk
Picks "Reactive" chip. Adds: "Only saw one dog from afar — handled it well but was tense." Flips Warn next walker toggle ON.

🦉 Owl owl_notes row, expires in 3 days — walks on Pebbles' card for every walker.
