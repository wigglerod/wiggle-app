What we are building
Wiggle is a self-running machine where owners book, agents resolve, Gen approves, walkers execute, and everyone sees the same truth — all day, every day, with as little manual work as possible.
It is not an app. It is not a spreadsheet. It is a loop. A booking triggers a chain of events that ends with a dog walked, a note sent, and billing updated — automatically, without anyone emailing anyone.
The technology serves the relationships. The relationships are what Wiggle actually is.

Every walk event. Pickup, return, not walking, activity note, flag. The complete walk history.

Timed staff notes. Expire automatically. Sector-filtered so only the right walkers see them.

74 rows. Owner booking name → dog name. The translation table that makes Mini Gen work.

84 rows. 29 must-ask dogs, 55 self-book dogs. Gen contacts must-ask weekly.
dog_conflicts
Dogs that cannot walk together. Mochi ↔ Chaska. Mini Gen checks this every run.

The symbiotic relationship — how it feeds itself
Every walk makes the next one easier.
This is not a one-way flow. Every node feeds every other node. That is the design.
👤

Acuity. Name goes into the system.
🤖

🏰

3 clicks. Week is clean. Exceptions handled.

🦉

Loop back ↺
Walk history → Mini Gen uses it → smarter drafts next week → Gen approves faster → walkers walk better-prepared → more notes → better history → repeat

Billing — how money flows automatically (Phase 3)
Step 1
Walk happens
Walker marks dog as picked up and returned. walker_notes row written.
Step 2
Counter decrements
Billing table in Supabase tracks walks remaining per dog. Each walk = -1.
Step 3
Tower flags low packages
Tower Billing tab shows red for 0 walks, amber for ≤2. Gen sees it immediately.
Step 4
Beast sends nudge
Gen tells Beast: "send renewal nudge to Raku." Beast confirms. Sends. Done.
Step 5
Owner renews
Pays. Counter resets. No gap in service. No awkward follow-up call.
What this is and what it is not
✕
Not a scheduling app A scheduling app just stores bookings. Wiggle takes bookings and turns them into walk groups, resolves name mismatches, checks conflicts, and puts the right dogs in front of the right walkers automatically.
✓
A self-running operations engine Gen approves. She doesn't build. The system builds. She manages exceptions. That's the shift — from doing the work to reviewing the work.
✕
Not two separate tools Tower and the app are not a desktop app and a mobile app that happen to share data. They are one system viewed from two angles. Every row written in one surface is instantly visible in the other.
✓
One truth, two windows Supabase is the only source of truth. The app is the field window. Tower is the desk window. Change anything in either place — the other sees it in under a second.
✕
Not automation for automation's sake Mini Gen doesn't send walkers anywhere. The Beast doesn't write to the database until a human confirms. Every autonomous action stops at a human checkpoint. The walkers and Gen stay in control.
✓
Technology that serves the relationship 8 years of client trust wasn't built by software. It was built by walkers who knew every dog by name. The app removes friction so walkers can focus on the dog in front of them — not their phone.
Wiggle is a small business that runs like a large one — because the system does the admin work, and the people do the work that only people can do.
